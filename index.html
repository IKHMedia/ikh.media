History of IKHMedia

In 2015 we started as the first community based station operating under the name Ian Broadcasting Networks.

The station began by re-broadcasting AM 1170 and AM 1430 in Waupun on 95.9 FM and 101.3 FM.

The station operated in the basement of 181 Harmsen Ave in Waupun as a high school student.

On August 12th 2016 we renamed to IKHNetworks and we moved from Harmsen Ave into a newly constructed house located on Dogleg Lane in Waupun.

In 2019 we moved from Waupun and we landed in Fond Du Lac. So I could attend classes at Moraine Park Technical College

In 2019 at the Rosalind Apartments we began broadcasting on 100.7 FM until October 1st 2021 when KFIZ began their FM station on 100.7 FM.

In 2020 We moved from The Rosalind To The Macy Street Apartments by the Lutheran Home in Fond Du Lac.

We were on 98.75 FM which appeared to cause a loss to Great 98 based on a written letter we got from Radio Plus.

In October 2021 we began operating on 93.7 FM in Waupun as I moved from FDL to Waupun.

While I was at 355 1/2 Doty Street for one month in 2021

We ended up renaming as IKHRadio

In 2022 I moved from Fox Lake Road in Waupun to The Exchange @ 104 in Fond Du Lac.

I was married for under 6 months, during the marriage we rebranded to KoLee Networks.

We were on 88.3 FM, 100.7 FM, 90.5 FM, 92.7 FM, 96.7 FM and even 98.3 FM.

In 2022 On New Years Day 2023 we had a stunt with Weird Al on the radio and on January 3rd 2023 KoLee Networking became IKHMedia.

We eventually ended up in 2023 having to shutdown our full power stations in Fond Du lac.

We moved in June 2023 to Eau Claire Wisconsin, and then moved to Tomah Wisconsin in November 2023.

On March 20th 2025 IKHMedia moved from Tomah to Whitewater Wisconsin.

On August 31st 2025 - IKHMedia rebranded as IKH dot Media

We're now online at ikh.media, yes, this is largest rebrand since April 20th 2023.

On August 31st 2025 UW Whitewater and IKH.Media began working together including a transfer of the HD Radio licenses for WSUW (91.7 FM) to IKH.Media while UW Whitewater remains the license holder for 91.7 The Edge

IKH.Media plans to keep 91.7 FM the exact same way as it has been. However the station will have HD Radio and RDS turned on

RDS Provides you with Artist and Song Titles on Your Car Radio Display. While HD Radio offers you 4 flavors of 91.7 FM.

Similar to ResNet - on the College Campus - we will have subchannels - for example you'll see 91.7-1, 91.7-2 91.7-3, 91.7-4.

If you don't have HD Radio - there's 3 low powered fm stations that will all operate at 75 watts - those low power stations will rebroadcast the HD Radio channels to regular FM radio so you will never have to be left out.
